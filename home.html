About Me

Teaching Philosophy

Training to be a secondary teacher in Technology and Applied Studies, my goal is to inspire students to thrive in a technology-driven world, focusing on hands-on, project-based learning experiences that connect theory with real-world applications. By fostering critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration, I believe that teachers need to equip students with the skills and confidence to innovate and excel.
